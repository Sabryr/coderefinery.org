We organize a road-show of hands-on informal and interactive three-day workshops across the Nordics with type-along type of presentations, live coding and demos. Short tutorials alternate with practical exercises. If you are interested in these workshops, please get in touch!

The format of CodeRefinery workshops with live demonstrations and interactive learning places some constraints on the workshop venue. We have therefore prepared a list of practical requirements.
